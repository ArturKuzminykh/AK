About me

I am a BIM practitioner with experience in modelling and coordination. It all started in 2017, when I was first introduced to Dynamo during an internship. Since then, inspired by the possibilities of the BIM methodology, I have been involved in various projects, including my outsourcing practice and academic programmes. I have a diploma with honours in Civil Engineering. Recently I have completed the European BIM A+ Master.

What I do

I work with BIM objects and models, shop drawings, point clouds, and standartization documents. I found my interest in optimising BIM workflows (modelling, coordination, management). I like exploring new things. After completing my master's dissertation, I became interested in the BIM in Web approach and software development for information management in collaborative project delivery.
